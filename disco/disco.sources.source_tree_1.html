disable_loadshapes_redirect() → None[source]¶

To comment out ‘Redirect LoadShapes.dss’ in Master.dss file

Redirect LoadShapes.dss is time consuming during OpenDSS loads the feeder, which make the PV deployments take a long to run. However, LoadShapes is not in use during PV deployments. To speed up, we’ll comment out the redirect and revert it back after PV deployments finished.
